About Blake Davies

Blake Davies provides a quick and simple solution for businesses that want to get a online without the headache of choosing between all the available website builders and hosting options that can confuse even the most tech savvy. From the intial design work and coding of your website, all the way through to hosting it live on the internet, Blake Davies offers a complete website solution. So before you spend your valuable time weighing up the pros and cons of the various online website buidlers and hosting services, get in touch with Blake Davies to see how simple getting a website can be.

A Website is an important tool that can empower any business in several ways. Most people these days will use the internet to research places and products prior to making a purchase or decision. Even if they don't buy your product online they are still likely to research it online first anyway. So if you don't have a website for your business you are missing out on a lot of potential business. A website is also very important to establish credibility as a business, regardless of its size. In a time where everything is online in some form of social media or webpage, a strong online presence is critical for any business.

Blake Davies specialises in single page websites for businesses of any size. With a growing number of people accessing the internet from mobile devices on the go, a single page website offers a pleaseant and easy experience for all the user. All they need to do is simply scroll down the page to find the information they are seeking without the hassle of having to click numerous times on a small screen. Avoid visitors to your website getting lost in multiple layers of navigation or stuck in an endless search for what they need.

Single page websites naturally guide visitors to browse through the content in a predetermined order which gives you more control over what inormation is provided to the user before they move on to the next section of your website. Some of the most unique and beautiful websites on the internet are single page websites. Operating within the constraints of a single page allows more room to get creative with the layout of content and think outside the box when it comes to delivering this content to the user. Unlike multiple page websites, which small amounts of content across seperate pages can leave the website looking empty or unfinished, a single page website caters to any volume of information without sacrificing appearances.

All of Blake Davies' website are fully responsive across a variety of devices from small mobile devices, to large desktop computer. Unlike some online DYI website builders, Blake Davies knows the ins and outs to ensuring your website will be displayed in all its beauty across a range of devices and operating devices. In addition to this, all of Blake Davies' websites are tested in multiple browsers to ensure that all coding will hold up as intended whether its viewed in Internet Explorer or Google Chrome. So have peace of mind by getting a professional to handle all the technical bits of your website while you do what you do best, and continue growing your business.

Blake Davies aims to not just be a complete website solution, but a business that helps and empowers other businesses to strive and grow. While specialising in Web Development, Graphic Design, and Hosting Services, Blake Davies does also provide consultative advice in all things business. So if you need advice on your marketing strategy and content, or just want to run an idea past someone who can offer quality feedback and cares about your business like its their own, you've come to the right place.
